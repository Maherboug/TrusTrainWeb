About Us

TrusTrain is a decentralized platform that facilitates collaboration between AI model trainers and resource providers. Trainers can upload their models and datasets, while resource providers train the models and return the updated weights in exchange for FLC tokens.

How TrusTrain Works

The platform helps achieve two key factors: it benefits money seekers by enabling them to provide accurate datasets or resources to generate stable income, advancing the use of cryptocurrency. More importantly, it helps generate more accurate models while eliminating data leakage and addressing hardware resource vertical scaling challenges. The platform also provides a component that aggregates weights from clients to generate a global AI model within a federated framework, enabling collaborative training while ensuring that data privacy is maintained, as client data is never directly shared. Leveraging blockchain technology, the platform implements smart contracts, allowing trainers and resource providers to securely exchange models, datasets, and training weights. This decentralized approach ensures transparency and automates payments using FLC tokens, which act as the medium of exchange between participants.
